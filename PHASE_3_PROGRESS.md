## ‚ú® New Tools Implemented (Phase 3A)

### Financial Tools (6 new)

1. **Budget Planner** (`/tools/budget-planner`)
   - Track income and expenses by category
   - Set budgets vs. actual spending
   - Real-time budget utilization tracking
   - Visual progress bars per category
   - Over-budget warnings
   - Savings rate calculation
   - **Features**: Add/remove categories, income/expense separation, summary metrics

2. **Debt Payoff Calculator** (`/tools/debt-payoff-calculator`)
   - Calculate debt payoff timeline
   - Compare minimum vs. accelerated payment plans
   - Shows total interest paid
   - Time and money saved by paying extra
   - Payoff date estimation
   - **Strategies**: Debt avalanche and snowball methods explained

3. **Emergency Fund Calculator** (`/tools/emergency-fund-calculator`)
   - Calculate recommended emergency fund size
   - 3-6-9-12 month coverage options
   - Progress tracking toward goal
   - Risk assessment (low/medium/high/critical)
   - Timeline to complete fund
   - **Guidelines**: Where to keep it, what qualifies as emergency

4. **Net Worth Calculator** (`/tools/net-worth-calculator`)
   - Track total assets vs. liabilities
   - Dynamic asset and liability lists
   - Real-time net worth calculation
   - Debt-to-asset ratio
   - Asset coverage percentage
   - **Categories**: Real estate, vehicles, savings, loans, mortgages, etc.

5. **Savings Goal Calculator** (`/tools/savings-goal-calculator`)
   - Calculate time to reach savings goals
   - Factor in compound interest
   - Shows required monthly deposit for target date
   - Interest earned projections
   - **Tips**: Automation, high-yield accounts, pay-yourself-first

6. **ROI Calculator** (`/tools/roi-calculator`)
   - Calculate Return on Investment
   - Annualized ROI for multi-year investments
   - Factor in additional costs/fees
   - Performance rating (Poor/Fair/Good/Excellent)
   - **Benchmarks**: S&P 500, real estate, savings accounts
   - Useful for: stocks, real estate, business investments

### Productivity Tools (1 new)

7. **Pomodoro Timer** (`/tools/pomodoro-timer`)
   - 25-minute work sessions
   - 5-minute short breaks
   - 15-minute long breaks (every 4 sessions)
   - Visual circular progress indicator
   - Session counter
   - **Benefits**: Improves focus, reduces burnout, tracks productivity

### Health & Wellness Tools (1 new)

8. **Sleep Calculator** (`/tools/sleep-calculator`)
   - Calculate optimal bedtime based on wake time
   - Calculate optimal wake time based on sleep time
   - Based on 90-minute sleep cycles
   - Accounts for 14 minutes to fall asleep
   - Shows options for 4, 5, and 6 cycles (6h, 7.5h, 9h)
   - **Tips**: Sleep hygiene, cycle science explanation

### Travel & Lifestyle Tools (1 new)

9. **Time Zone Converter** (`/tools/time-zone-converter`)
   - Convert times between 16 major time zones
   - Shows current time in all zones
   - World clock display
   - **Zones**: US (ET, CT, MT, PT, AK, HI), Europe, Asia, Australia
   - Perfect for: remote work, international meetings, travel planning
